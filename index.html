Analysis of British airways
customers' reviews using python, and SQL Server

In this project, I dug deep to understanding BA customers' feelings and needs in order to help BA with their business decisions, and a better repositioning of their services.

For me to be able to achieve that, I extracted customers' sentiments (Positive, Neutral, Negative) from their reviews employing NLTK Model. For me to also understand their needs, I employed Topic modelling to generate topics from their reviews using BERTopic model. All these were done in Python.
Data Cleaning was then carried out in SQL Server, where Exploratory Data Analysis was also done.
