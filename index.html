Analysis of British airways
customers' reviews using python, and SQL Server

The nagging question for BA is how to stay above the competition curve? British Airways has been in Business for 50 years (1974 to current). Over these years more than 1000 airlines have sprung up and are in operation. Within these numbers, more than 30 airlines are major global carriers and are in direct competition with BA. BA, like every businesses understands that customers are king. Even though BA leadership might believe they have good products and services, do their customers feel same way?

For BA to remain above the competition curve, they must understand their customers' sentiment towards them. In this project, therefore, I employed NLTK in python to extract customers' sentiment scores (Positive, Neutral, Negative) to understand BA customers' loyalty towards the airline. I also employed Topic modelling to generate topics that summarizes BA customers' feelings and attitude towards the brand using BERTopic model, still in python. Data Cleaning was then carried out in SQL Server, where Exploratory Data Analysis was also done. The data findings were then visiualized in Power BI


What then does this data reveal, would BA shutdown soon, or should they fire their CEO? Read to find out.
